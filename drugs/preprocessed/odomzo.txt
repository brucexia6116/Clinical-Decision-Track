Common side effects of Odomzo include: muscle spasm, musculoskeletal pain, and myalgia.
See below for a comprehensive list of adverse effects.
As well as its needed effects, sonidegib (the active ingredient contained in Odomzo) may cause unwanted side effects that require medical attention.

Some sonidegib side effects may not need any medical attention.
As your body gets used to the medicine these side effects may disappear.
