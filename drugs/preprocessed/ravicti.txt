Common side effects of Ravicti include: dizziness, fatigue, headache, nausea, and vomiting.
See below for a comprehensive list of adverse effects.
As well as its needed effects, glycerol phenylbutyrate (the active ingredient contained in Ravicti) may cause unwanted side effects that require medical attention.

Some glycerol phenylbutyrate side effects may not need any medical attention.
As your body gets used to the medicine these side effects may disappear.
