Commonly reported side effects of cinacalcet include: decreased plasma testosterone, diarrhea, dizziness, myalgia, nausea, vomiting, and hypoparathyroidism.
Other side effects include: dialysis access infection, asthenia, chest pain, hypertension, hypotension, and anorexia.
