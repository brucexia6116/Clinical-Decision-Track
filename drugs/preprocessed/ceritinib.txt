Commonly reported side effects of ceritinib include: abdominal pain, diarrhea, hyperglycemia, increased serum alanine aminotransferase, nausea, and vomiting.
Other side effects include: interstitial pulmonary disease, pneumonitis, and prolonged qt interval on ecg.
See below for a comprehensive list of adverse effects.
The most common side effect resulting in dose reductions or interruptions were increased ALT , nausea , increased AST , diarrhea , and vomiting .
The most common side effects leading to discontinuation were pneumonia, interstitial lung disease/pneumonitis, and decreased appetite.
