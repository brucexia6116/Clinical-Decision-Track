Gastrointestinal side effects have included nausea  and vomiting .
Cardiovascular side effects have included hypertension , chest wall rigidity , arrhythmias , bradycardia , tachycardia , and hypotension .
Rare cardiovascular reactions have included asystole and sinus arrest.
Musculoskeletal side effects have included skeletal muscle movements ( to ), and truncal rigidity, particularly of the neck and extremities (less than ).
Respiratory side effects have included apnea ( to ), postoperative respiratory depression (less than ), bronchospasm, hypercarbia, and laryngospasm (less than ).
Respiratory depression has been reversed with the opioid antagonist naloxone.
Nervous system side effects have included dizziness ( to ), headache, myoclonic and dystonic movements, confusion, euphoria, and shivering (less than ).
Hypersensitivity side effects including anaphylaxis have been reported rarely .
Dermatologic side effects have included pruritus and urticaria (less than ).
Pruritus may occur with greater frequency in patients receiving epidural alfentanil (the active ingredient contained in Alfenta) (up to  in one study).
Pruritus during epidural infusions has been described as segmental (occurring on the chest) versus the face and arms, which is often seen with morphine.
Diphenhydramine is effective in relieving pruritus associated with opioids.
Local side effects, such as injection site pain (less than ) have been reported.
