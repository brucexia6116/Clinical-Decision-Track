As well as its needed effects, tetracaine ophthalmic (the active ingredient contained in TetraVisc) may cause unwanted side effects that require medical attention.
It is possible that some side effects of TetraVisc may not have been reported.
These can be reported to the FDA here.
Always consult a healthcare professional for medical advice.
Blurred vision
redness of the clear part of the eye
sensitivity to light
severe stinging in the eye
tearing
throbbing eye pain
Bloody eye
burning, stinging, itching, redness, or irritation of the eye
change in vision
