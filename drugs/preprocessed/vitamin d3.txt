As well as its needed effects, cholecalciferol (the active ingredient contained in Vitamin D) may cause unwanted side effects that require medical attention.
Metabolic side effects have included hypercalcemia.
Uncommon ( to ): Hypercalciuria
Rare (less than ): Pruritus, rash, urticaria
Frequency not reported: Hypersensitivity reactions such as angioedema or laryngeal edema
Frequency not reported: Nausea, vomiting
It is possible that some side effects of Vitamin D may not have been reported.
These can be reported to the FDA here.
Always consult a healthcare professional for medical advice.
