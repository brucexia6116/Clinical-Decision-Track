Get emergency medical help if you have any of these signs of an allergic reaction while taking milk thistle:  hives; difficult breathing; swelling of your face, lips, tongue, or throat.
Common side effects may include:
indigestion, bloating, gas, stomach pain;
diarrhea; or
nausea, loss of appetite.
This is not a complete list of side effects and others may occur.
