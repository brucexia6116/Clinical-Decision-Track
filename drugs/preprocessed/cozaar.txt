Common side effects of Cozaar include: asthenia, chest pain, diarrhea, fatigue, and hypoglycemia.
Other side effects include: hyperkalemia, hypotension, and orthostatic hypotension.
See below for a comprehensive list of adverse effects.
The most common adverse reactions were upper respiratory infection and dizziness.
