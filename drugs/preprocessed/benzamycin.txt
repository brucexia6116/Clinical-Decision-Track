Local effects are the most commonly reported adverse effects of benzoyl peroxide-erythromycin topical.
Transient drying occurs most often, followed by erythema, pruritus, burning, tenderness, and desquamation.
A single case of generalized urticarial reaction to erythromycin topical has been reported.
Allergic sensitization to benzoyl peroxide has been reported to occur in 1% to 5% of patients.
