Common side effects of Tygacil include: nausea and vomiting.
Other side effects include: infection and hypoproteinemia.
See below for a comprehensive list of adverse effects.
As well as its needed effects, tigecycline (the active ingredient contained in Tygacil) may cause unwanted side effects that require medical attention.

Some tigecycline side effects may not need any medical attention.
As your body gets used to the medicine these side effects may disappear.
