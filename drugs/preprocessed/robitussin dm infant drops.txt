Check with your doctor if any of these most COMMON side effects persist or become bothersome:
Dizziness; drowsiness; stomach upset.
Severe allergic reactions (rash; hives; itching; difficulty breathing; tightness in the chest; swelling of the mouth, face, lips, or tongue).
Nervous system side effects of dextromethorphan have included drowsiness and dizziness.
Adverse effects, which have occurred at higher doses, include excitation, mental confusion, and opioid like respiratory depression.
In some cases of abuse, patients have experienced euphoria, hyperactivity, mania, and auditory and visual hallucinations.
Gastrointestinal side effects have included nausea, vomiting and constipation.
Dermatologic side effects have included pruritus and rash.
Hypersensitivity side effects of dextromethorphan have included fixed-drug eruptions.
It is possible that some side effects of Robitussin Cough + Chest Congestion DM may not have been reported.
These can be reported to the FDA here.
