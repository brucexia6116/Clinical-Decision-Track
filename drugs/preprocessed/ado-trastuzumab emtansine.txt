Commonly reported side effects of ado-trastuzumab emtansine include: increased serum bilirubin, increased serum transaminases, cough, decreased platelet count, dyspnea, fatigue, fever, peripheral neuropathy, and thrombocytopenia.
Other side effects include: chills.
