As well as its needed effects, econazole topical (the active ingredient contained in Spectazole) may cause unwanted side effects that require medical attention.
If any of the following side effects occur while taking econazole topical, check with your doctor immediately:

Topical Cream:-Common ( to ): Pain/stinging-Uncommon ( to ): Discomfort, swelling
Vaginal Cream and Pessaries:-Uncommon ( to ): Vulvovaginal burning sensation
One patient developed erythematous, edematous, urticarial-like lesions with vesiculation at the site of drug application.
Sensitivity to this drug was confirmed by patch testing.
