Common side effects of Entocort EC include: acne vulgaris, moon face, and bruise.
Other side effects include: ankle edema, and hirsutism.
See below for a comprehensive list of adverse effects.
Other dosage forms:
The most commonly reported adverse events with inhaled use have included nasopharyngitis, nasal congestion pharyngitis, rhinitis, and oral candidiasis.
Headache, nausea, abdominal pain, and fatigue have been commonly reported with oral and rectal use.
Due to the nature of its administration (inhalation, gastro-protective oral formulations, and rectal enema), it is less likely to cause systemic adverse effects associated with the use of corticosteroids.
Epidemiological studies have shown an increased incidence of pneumonia in patients with chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD) treated with inhaled corticosteroids.
