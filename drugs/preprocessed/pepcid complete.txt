Get emergency medical help if you have any of these signs of an allergic reaction while taking calcium carbonate / famotidine / magnesium hydroxide:  hives; difficult breathing; swelling of your face, lips, tongue, or throat.
Stop taking this medication and call your doctor at once if:
your heartburn gets worse; or
you still have heartburn after  weeks of taking famotidine, calcium, and magnesium.
