Common side effects of Advair Diskus include: upper respiratory tract infection, pneumonia, and headache.
Other side effects include: dizziness, myalgia, and nausea.
See below for a comprehensive list of adverse effects.
The most common events that occurred more frequently were nasopharyngitis, upper respiratory tract infection, nasal congestion, back pain, sinusitis, dizziness, nausea, pneumonia, candidiasis, and dysphonia.
