Common side effects of Avonex include: flu-like symptoms, fever, headache, myalgia, nausea, and chills.
Other side effects include: anemia.
See below for a comprehensive list of adverse effects.
Influenza-like symptoms (, myalgia, fever, chills, sweating, asthenia, headache, nausea) are the most common adverse reactions.
