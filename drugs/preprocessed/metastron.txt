Along with its needed effects, strontium- chloride (the active ingredient contained in Metastron) may cause some unwanted effects.
When strontium chloride Sr  is used at recommended doses, side effects usually are rare.
However, blood problems, such as a decrease in the number of white blood cells or platelets, may occur in some patients.
