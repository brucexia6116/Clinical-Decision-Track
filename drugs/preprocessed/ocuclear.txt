As well as its needed effects, oxymetazoline ophthalmic (the active ingredient contained in OcuClear) may cause unwanted side effects that require medical attention.

When this medicine is used for short periods of time at low doses, side effects usually are rare.
If any of the following side effects occur while taking oxymetazoline ophthalmic, check with your doctor or nurse as soon as possible:
It is possible that some side effects of OcuClear may not have been reported.
These can be reported to the FDA here.
Always consult a healthcare professional for medical advice.
Increase in irritation or redness of eyes
Fast, irregular, or pounding heartbeat
headache or lightheadedness
nervousness
trembling
trouble in sleeping
