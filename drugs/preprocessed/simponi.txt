Some golimumab side effects may not need any medical attention.
As your body gets used to the medicine these side effects may disappear.

The most common side effects were upper respiratory tract infection, sepsis, increased ALT, increased AST, tuberculosis, and anemia.
The most serious side effects were serious infections and malignancies.
