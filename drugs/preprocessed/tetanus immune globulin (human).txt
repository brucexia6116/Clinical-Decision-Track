As well as its needed effects, tetanus immune globulin may cause unwanted side effects that require medical attention.
Local side effects have included soreness, pain, and tenderness at the injection site.
Renal side effects have included rare cases of nephrotic syndrome.
Hypersensitivity reactions have included rare cases of angioneurotic edema and anaphylactic shock.
Other side effects have included slight temperature elevation.
It is possible that some side effects of tetanus immune globulin may not have been reported.
These can be reported to the FDA here.
