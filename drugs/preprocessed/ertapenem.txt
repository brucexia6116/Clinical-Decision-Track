In general, the most common side effects associated with ertapenem have been considered mild to moderate and have included diarrhea , infused vein complication , nausea , headache , and vaginitis in females .
Small intestinal obstruction, C difficile infection or colitis, dry mouth, and hematochezia were reported in a study using  g of ertapenem for prophylaxis of surgical site infection following elective colorectal surgery.
