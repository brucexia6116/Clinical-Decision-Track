Commonly reported side effects of clobazam include: constipation, drowsiness, fever, sedation, aggressive behavior, ataxia, lethargy, drooling, and irritability.
Other side effects include: pneumonia, urinary tract infection, cough, bronchitis, dysarthria, dysphagia, fatigue, insomnia, decreased appetite, and increased appetite.
