Common side effects of Fenoglide include: increased serum aspartate aminotransferase and increased serum alanine aminotransferase.
Other side effects include: abnormal hepatic function tests, increased creatine phosphokinase, and increased serum transaminases.
See below for a comprehensive list of adverse effects.
The most frequently reported side effects were abnormal liver tests, AST increased, ALT increased, creatine phosphokinase (CPK) increased, and rhinitis.
