Renal side effects including urinary tract infection  have been reported.
General side effects including insomnia  and asthenia  have been reported.
Nervous system side effects including dizziness , headache , balance disorder , multiple sclerosis relapse , and paresthesia  have been reported.
Gastrointestinal side effects including nausea , constipation , dyspepsia , and pharyngolaryngeal pain  have been reported.
Musculoskeletal side effects including back pain  have been reported.
Respiratory side effects including nasopharyngitis  have been reported.
Genitourinary side effects have been have reported in animal studies which noted a significant increase in uterine polyps at the highest dose tested.
