Common side effects of Dacogen include: neutropenia and thrombocytopenia.
See below for a comprehensive list of adverse effects.
If any of the following side effects occur while taking decitabine, check with your doctor or nurse immediately:
Hematologic side effects including neutropenia (up to ), thrombocytopenia (up to ), anemia (up to ), febrile neutropenia (up to ), leukopenia (up to ), lymphadenopathy , pancytopenia , and thrombocythemia  have been reported.
General side effects including pyrexia , fatigue , pyrexia , peripheral edema (up to ), rigors , edema (up to ), chills , asthenia , pain , lethargy , tenderness , mucosal inflammation , fall , chest discomfort , intermittent pyrexia , chest pain , malaise , pain , and crepitations  have been reported.
Gastrointestinal side effects including nausea (up to ), constipation (up to ), diarrhea (up to ), vomiting (up to ), abdominal pain , oral mucosal petechiae , stomatitis (up to ), dyspepsia (up to ), ascites , gingival bleeding , hemorrhoids , loose stools , tongue ulceration , dysphagia (up to ), oral soft tissue disorder , toothache , abdominal pain upper (up to ), lip ulceration , abdominal distension , gastroesophageal reflux disease , oral pain , and glossodynia  have been reported.
Respiratory side effects including cough (up to ), dyspnea , pharyngitis , lung crackles , epistaxis , decreased breath sounds , hypoxia , pharyngolaryngeal pain , rales , pleural effusion , sinus congestion , and postnasal drip  have been reported.
Other side effects have included vascular disorders such as petechiae , pallor , and hematoma .
Other side effects such as laboratory abnormalities including increased blood alkaline phosphatase , increased aspartate aminotransferase , increased blood urea , decreased weight ( ), contusions , increased blood lactate dehydrogenase , decreased blood albumin , increased blood bilirubin ( ), increased blood bicarbonate , decreased blood chloride , ear pain , abnormal breath sounds ( ), decreased protein total , decreased blood bicarbonate , and decreased blood bilirubin  have also been reported.
Metabolic side effects including hyperglycemia (up to ), hypoalbuminemia , hypomagnesemia (up to ), anorexia (up to ), hypokalemia (up to ), hyponatremia , decreased appetite (up to ), hyperkalemia , and dehydration (up to ) have been reported.
Nervous system side effects including headache (up to ), dizziness (up to ), and hypoesthesia  have been reported.
Psychiatric side effects including insomnia (up to ), confusional state (up to ), anxiety (up to ) and depression  have been reported.
Immunologic side effects have included infections and infestations such as pneumonia , cellulitis , candidal infection , catheter related infection , urinary tract infection , staphylococcal infection , oral candidiasis , sinusitis , and bacteremia .
Dermatologic side effects including ecchymosis (up to ), rash , erythema (up to ), petechiae , skin lesion , pruritus , rash , alopecia , dry skin , urticaria , facial swelling , night sweats , and cases of Sweet's syndrome (acute febrile neutrophilic dermatosis) have been reported.
Musculoskeletal side effects including arthralgia (up to ), limb pain , back pain (up to ), pain in extremity , myalgia (up to ), chest wall pain , muscle spasms , musculoskeletal discomfort , bone pain , muscular weakness , and musculoskeletal pain  have been reported.
Cardiovascular side effects including cardiac murmur , hypotension (up to ), tachycardia , hypertension , pulmonary edema  and congestive cardiac failure  have been reported.
Hepatic side effects including hyperbilirubinemia  have been reported.
Hypersensitivity side effects including transfusion reactions  have been reported.
Ocular side effects including blurred vision  have been reported.
Genitourinary side effects including dysuria  and urinary frequency  have been reported.
Local side effects including catheter site erythema , catheter site pain , and injection site swelling  have been reported.
