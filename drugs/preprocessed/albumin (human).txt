Side effects include:
Anaphylactoid reactions,    fever,               chills,               rash,       nausea,            vomiting,          tachycardia,         hypotension.

Dermatologic side effects have included urticaria, skin rash, pruritus, edema, and erythema.
Nervous system side effects have included headache, chills, and febrile reactions.
Cardiovascular side effects have included hypotension.
Gastrointestinal side effects have included nausea, vomiting and increased salivation.
Respiratory side effects have included bronchospasm.
