Common side effects of Jadenu include: abdominal pain, cough, diarrhea, fever, headache, increased serum creatinine, influenza, nasopharyngitis, nausea, proteinuria, vomiting, and pharyngolaryngeal pain.
Other side effects include: otic infection, respiratory tract infection, arthralgia, back pain, bronchitis, fatigue, increased serum alanine aminotransferase, pharyngitis, rhinitis, skin rash, tonsillitis, and upper abdominal pain.
See below for a comprehensive list of adverse effects.
The most common adverse events were abdominal pain, nausea, vomiting, diarrhea, skin rashes, and increases in serum creatinine.
Gastrointestinal symptoms, increases in serum creatinine, and skin rash were dose related.
