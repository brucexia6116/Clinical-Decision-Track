Commonly reported side effects of cabozantinib include: diarrhea, hypocalcemia, hypertension, and palmar-plantar erythrodysesthesia.
Other side effects include: venous thromboembolism, asthenia, fatigue, and decreased appetite.
See below for a comprehensive list of adverse effects.
