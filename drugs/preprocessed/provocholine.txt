Check with your doctor if any of these most COMMON side effects persist or become bothersome:
Headache; itching; lightheadedness; throat irritation.
Severe allergic reactions (rash; hives; itching; difficulty breathing; tightness in the chest; swelling of the mouth, face, lips, or tongue); chest pain; cough; fainting; severe dizziness; wheezing.
When administered orally or by injection, this drug is reported to cause nausea, vomiting, substernal pain or pressure, hypotension, fainting, and transient complete heart block.
Overdosage with this drug, when administered orally or by injection, can result in a syncopal reaction with cardiac arrest and loss of consciousness.
This drug should be administered only by inhalation.
Very rare (less than ): Headache, lightheadedness
Very rare (less than ): Itching
