Common side effects of Betaseron include: uterine hemorrhage, abdominal pain, antibody development, asthenia, chest pain, decreased white blood cell count, fever, flu-like symptoms, headache, hypertonia, increased serum alanine aminotransferase, injection site reaction, insomnia, leukopenia, myalgia, pain, anxiety, arthralgia, constipation, diarrhea, disorder of integument, dizziness, dyspepsia, lymphocytopenia, myasthenia, nausea, peripheral edema, skin rash, urinary urgency, abnormal absolute neutrophil count, chills, and inflammation at injection site.
Other side effects include: alopecia, dyspnea, hypertension, leg cramps, lymphadenopathy, palpitations, tachycardia, tissue necrosis at injection site, impotence, nervousness, peripheral vascular disease, urinary frequency, vasodilatation, weight gain, malaise, and diaphoresis.
