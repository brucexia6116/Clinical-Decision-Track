As well as its needed effects, dexamethasone ophthalmic (the active ingredient contained in Ozurdex) may cause unwanted side effects that require medical attention.
If any of the following side effects occur while taking dexamethasone ophthalmic, check with your doctor or nurse immediately:
The most commonly reported side effects were cataract, elevation of intraocular pressure, and conjunctival hemorrhage.
