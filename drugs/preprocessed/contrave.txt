Common side effects of Contrave include: constipation, dizziness, headache, insomnia, nausea, and vomiting.
Other side effects include: anxiety, diarrhea, increased blood pressure, tremor, depression, hypertension, and xerostomia.
See below for a comprehensive list of adverse effects.
