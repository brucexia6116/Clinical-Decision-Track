Commonly reported side effects of exenatide include: diarrhea, nausea, and vomiting.
Other side effects include: dizziness, dyspepsia, fidgeting, and headache.
See below for a comprehensive list of adverse effects.
The most commonly reported side effects were: anti-exenatide antibodies, nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea.
Postmarketing reports: Generalized pruritus and/or urticaria, macular or papular rash, angioedema, and anaphylactic reaction
