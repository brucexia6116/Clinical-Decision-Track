As well as its needed effects, beclomethasone nasal (the active ingredient contained in Vancenase AQ) may cause unwanted side effects that require medical attention.
If any of the following side effects occur while taking beclomethasone nasal, check with your doctor immediately:
The most commonly reported side effects were epistaxis, nasal discomfort, throat irritation, and headache.
Postmarketing reports: Hypersensitivity reactions (including anaphylaxis, angioedema, rash, urticaria, and bronchospasm)
