Common side effects of Esbriet include: abdominal distention, abdominal pain, diarrhea, dizziness, dyspepsia, fatigue, gastroesophageal reflux disease, nausea, skin rash, upper abdominal pain, vomiting, weight loss, and anorexia.
Other side effects include: pruritus, skin photosensitivity, and decreased appetite.
