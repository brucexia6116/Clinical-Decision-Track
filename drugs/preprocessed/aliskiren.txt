Episodes of tonic-clonic seizures were reported in two patients treated with aliskiren in the clinical trials.
One patient had predisposing causes for seizures and a negative electroencephalogram (EEG) and cerebral imaging following the seizures (the other patient's EEG and imaging results were not reported).
Aliskiren was discontinued and there was no rechallenge.
