Commonly reported side effects of bcg include: urinary tract infection, fever, hematuria, irritable bladder, urinary frequency, urinary urgency, vomiting, chills, and malaise.
Other side effects include: arthralgia.
See below for a comprehensive list of adverse effects.
Genitourinary side effects including dysuria , urinary frequency , hematuria , cystitis , urinary tract infection , urgency , genital pain , urinary incontinence , cramps , and contracted bladder  were most commonly reported.
Nervous system side effects including malaise  and arthralgia/myalgia  have been reported.
Hematologic side effects including anemia , leukopenia , and coagulopathy  have been reported.
Gastrointestinal side effects including nausea/vomiting , anorexia , diarrhea , and abdominal pain  have been reported.
General side effects including fever , chills , and headache  have been reported.
Immunologic side effects including systemic infection  and pulmonary infection  have been reported.
Four cases of immune reconstitution syndrome have also been reported.
Renal side effects including renal toxicity  have been reported.
Dermatologic side effects including skin rash  have been rarely reported.
Unclassified cardiovascular side effects were infrequently reported .
