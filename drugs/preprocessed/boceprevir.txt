Commonly reported side effects of boceprevir include: anemia, dizziness, dyspnea on exertion, and neutropenia.
See below for a comprehensive list of adverse effects.
This drug must be administered in combination with peginterferon alfa and ribavirin.
The manufacturer product information for peginterferon alfa and ribavirin should be consulted for associated side effects.

The side effects reported most frequently were fatigue, anemia, nausea, headache, and dysgeusia.

The side effects leading to discontinuation most often were anemia and fatigue.
