Minocycline topical (the active ingredient contained in Arestin) powder is intended for local application only.
Systemic effects from the absorption of small amounts of the drug are unlikely.
Other side effects have included headache  and pain .
Dermatologic side effects have included stomatitis , mouth ulceration , and mucous membrane disorder .
Gastrointestinal side effects have included dyspepsia .
Local side effects have included periodontitis , tooth disorder , tooth caries , dental pain , gingivitis , pharyngitis , and dental infection .
Immunologic side effects have included flu syndrome  and infection .
