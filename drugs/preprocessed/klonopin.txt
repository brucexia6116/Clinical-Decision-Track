Common side effects of Klonopin include: upper respiratory tract infection, ataxia, dizziness, and drowsiness.
Other side effects include: depression, dysarthria, fatigue, hypersensitivity reaction, increased cough, myalgia, nervousness, rhinitis, sinusitis, vaginitis, and reduced intellectual ability.
See below for a comprehensive list of adverse effects.
The most commonly reported side effects were drowsiness, ataxia, and behavioral problems.
