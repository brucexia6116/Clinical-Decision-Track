Get emergency medical help if you have any of these signs of an allergic reaction while taking starch topical:  hives; difficult breathing; swelling of your face, lips, tongue, or throat.
Stop using starch rectal and call your doctor at once if you have:
bloody diarrhea; or
severe rectal pain, bleeding, or irritation.
This is not a complete list of side effects and others may occur.
Call your doctor for medical advice about side effects.
It is possible that some side effects of starch topical may not have been reported.
These can be reported to the FDA here.
