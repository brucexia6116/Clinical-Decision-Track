As well as its needed effects, proparacaine ophthalmic (the active ingredient contained in Ophthetic) may cause unwanted side effects that require medical attention.
Some proparacaine ophthalmic side effects may not need any medical attention.
As your body gets used to the medicine these side effects may disappear.
