Hypersensitivity side effects have included allergy, rash, pruritus, and erythema, chills/rise in temperature, arthralgia, headache, elevated thrombin antithrombin levels, and an increase in hepatic enzymes.
Hematologic side effects have included factor XIII (the active ingredient contained in Corifact) inhibitor formation.
Cardiovascular side effects have included thrombosis and embolism.
General side effects have included flu-like syndrome and pyrexia.
Gastrointestinal side effects have included abdominal pain, diarrhea and vomiting.
Musculoskeletal side effects have included contusion, joint injury, limb injury, road traffic accident, and arthralgia.
Respiratory side effects have included upper respiratory tract infection and epistaxis.
Nervous system side effects have included headache and head injury.
Immunologic side effects have included allergic/anaphylactic reaction (including cutaneous reactions, alteration in blood pressure, nausea, dyspnea, fever, and chills).
