Very rare (less than ): Churg-Strauss Syndrome
Churg-Strauss syndrome is a rare granulomatous eosinophilic condition that involves the upper and lower airways and manifests as rhinitis, sinusitis and asthma.
If untreated the syndrome may progress to systemic vasculitis, peripheral neuropathy and potentially fatal cardiac complications.
In most cases, the condition emerged during withdrawal of oral corticosteroid therapy.
A causative role for leukotriene receptor antagonists has not been ruled out.
