Side effects include:
Sedation, sleepiness, dizziness, disturbed coordination, epigastric distress, thickening of bronchial secretions.
The most commonly reported side effects included somnolence, dizziness, and incoordination.
Common ( to ): Sedation/somnolence/sleepiness, drowsiness, unsteadiness, dizziness, headache, attention disturbanceRare ( to ): Extrapyramidal effects, tremor, convulsionsFrequency not reported: Paresthesia, dyskinesia/muscle dyskinesia, vertigo, neuritis, incoordination, psychomotor impairment, activation of epileptogenic foci
Drowsiness usually diminishes after a few days.
