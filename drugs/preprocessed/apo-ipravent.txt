Commonly reported side effects of ipratropium include: upper respiratory tract infection, urinary tract infection, bronchitis, exacerbation of chronic obstructive pulmonary disease, and sinusitis.
Other side effects include: cough, dyspepsia, and flu-like symptoms.
See below for a comprehensive list of adverse effects.
The most common adverse reactions were bronchitis, COPD exacerbation, dyspnea, headache, throat irritation, cough, dry mouth, gastro-intestinal motility disorders (including constipation, diarrhea and vomiting), nausea, and dizziness.
