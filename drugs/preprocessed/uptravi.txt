Common side effects of Uptravi include: diarrhea, headache, jaw pain, limb pain, myalgia, nausea, vomiting, and flushing.
Other side effects include: anemia, and decreased appetite.
See below for a comprehensive list of adverse effects.
As well as its needed effects, selexipag (the active ingredient contained in Uptravi) may cause unwanted side effects that require medical attention.
Some selexipag side effects may not need any medical attention.
As your body gets used to the medicine these side effects may disappear.
