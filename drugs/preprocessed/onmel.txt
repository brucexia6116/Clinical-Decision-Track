Common side effects of Onmel include: nausea.
Other side effects include: vomiting.
See below for a comprehensive list of adverse effects.
As well as its needed effects, itraconazole (the active ingredient contained in Onmel) may cause unwanted side effects that require medical attention.
Some itraconazole side effects may not need any medical attention.
As your body gets used to the medicine these side effects may disappear.
