Check with your doctor if any of these most COMMON side effects persist or become bothersome:
Constipation; diarrhea; dizziness; drowsiness; excitability; headache; loss of appetite; nausea; nervousness or anxiety; trouble sleeping; upset stomach; vomiting; weakness.
Severe allergic reactions (rash; hives; difficulty breathing; tightness in the chest; swelling of the mouth, face, lips, or tongue); difficulty urinating or inability to urinate; fast or irregular heartbeat; hallucinations; seizures; severe dizziness, lightheadedness, or headache; tremor; vision changes.
Cardiovascular side effects have included tachycardia, palpitations, pallor, arrhythmias, and cardiovascular collapse with hypotension.
Gastrointestinal side effects have included nausea.
Nervous system side effects have included headache, dizziness, fear, anxiety, tenseness, hallucinations, tremor, weakness, insomnia, convulsions, and central nervous system depression.
Respiratory side effects have included respiratory difficulty.
Genitourinary side effects have included dysuria and urinary retention.
