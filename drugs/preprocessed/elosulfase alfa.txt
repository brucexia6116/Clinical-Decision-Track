Commonly reported side effects of elosulfase alfa include: anaphylaxis, abdominal pain, fever, hypersensitivity reaction, nausea, and vomiting.
Other side effects include: cyanosis, chest discomfort, constriction of the pharynx, cough, dyspnea, gastrointestinal symptoms, hypotension, retching, skin rash, urticaria, erythema, and flushing.
