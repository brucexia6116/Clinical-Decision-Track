Other dosage forms:
No COMMON side effects have been reported with vitamin b6/vitamin b12/vitamin e/folic acid with minerals.
Seek medical attention right away if any of these SEVERE side effects occur while taking multivitamin with minerals (the active ingredient contained in Nicomide) 
Severe allergic reactions (rash; hives; itching; difficulty breathing; tightness in the chest; swelling of the mouth, face, lips, or tongue); burning, numbness, or tingling.
It is possible that some side effects of Nicomide may not have been reported.
These can be reported to the FDA here.
Always consult a healthcare professional for medical advice.
