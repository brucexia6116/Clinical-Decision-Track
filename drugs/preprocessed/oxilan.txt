The reported incidence of adverse reactions to contrast agents in patients with a history of allergy is twice that of the general population.
Patients with a history of previous reactions to a contrast agent are three times more susceptible than other patients.
However, sensitivity to contrast agents does not appear to increase with repeated examinations.
