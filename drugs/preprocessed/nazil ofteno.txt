As well as its needed effects, naphazoline ophthalmic (the active ingredient contained in Nazil Ofteno) may cause unwanted side effects that require medical attention.

Some naphazoline ophthalmic side effects may not need any medical attention.
As your body gets used to the medicine these side effects may disappear.
