Other dosage forms:
When used in small doses, no COMMON side effects have been reported with sodium fluoride (the active ingredient contained in Nafrinse) chewable tablets.
Seek medical attention right away if any of these SEVERE side effects occur while taking fluoride:
Severe allergic reactions (rash; hives; itching; difficulty breathing; tightness in the chest; swelling of the mouth, face, lips, or tongue).
It is possible that some side effects of Nafrinse may not have been reported.
These can be reported to the FDA here.
Always consult a healthcare professional for medical advice.
