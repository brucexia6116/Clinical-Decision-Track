Common side effects of Alphagan P include: blurred vision, burning sensation of eyes, drowsiness, eye pruritus, follicular conjunctivitis, headache, local ocular hypersensitivity reaction, ocular hyperemia, stinging of eyes, foreign body sensation, and xerostomia.
