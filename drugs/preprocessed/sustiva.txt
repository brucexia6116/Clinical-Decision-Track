Common side effects of Sustiva include: diarrhea, nausea, and skin rash.
Other side effects include: dizziness, lack of concentration, fatigue, headache, vomiting, and anorexia.
See below for a comprehensive list of adverse effects.
As well as its needed effects, efavirenz (the active ingredient contained in Sustiva) may cause unwanted side effects that require medical attention.

The most significant side effects included nervous system symptoms, psychiatric symptoms, and rash.
