Common side effects of Mavik include: cough, dizziness, and hypotension.
Other side effects include: cardiogenic shock, syncope, hyperkalemia, increased serum creatinine, and increased blood urea nitrogen.
See below for a comprehensive list of adverse effects.
The most frequently reported side effects were dizziness and a dry, nonproductive cough.
