A variety of CNS anticholinergic effects have been reported, including headache, confusion, hallucinations and somnolence.
Patients should be monitored for signs of anticholinergic CNS effects, particularly after beginning treatment or increasing the dose.
Ocular side effects have included abnormal vision ( or greater) and dry eyes.
General side effects have included flu syndrome , asthenia ( or greater), arthralgias, peripheral edema, and weight gain.
