Local side effects have included mild and transient burning and stinging usually associated with the severity of infestation.
Dermatological side effects have included pruritus , erythema ( to ), and rash ( to ).
Nervous system side effects have included numbness and tingling ( to ).
Headache and dizziness have also been reported.
Rare occurrences of seizure have been reported;  however, an association with the use of permethrin topical (the active ingredient contained in Elimite) has not been confirmed.
Gastrointestinal side effects have included abdominal pain, diarrhea, and nausea and/or vomiting.
General side effects have included fever.
