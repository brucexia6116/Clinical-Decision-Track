As well as its needed effects, oxymetazoline nasal (the active ingredient contained in Zicam Sinus Relief) may cause unwanted side effects that require medical attention.
If any of the following side effects occur while taking oxymetazoline nasal, check with your doctor immediately:
Some oxymetazoline nasal side effects may not need any medical attention.
As your body gets used to the medicine these side effects may disappear.
Your health care professional may be able to help you prevent or reduce these side effects, but do check with them if any of the following side effects continue, or if you are concerned about them:
Very rare (less than ): Tachycardia, palpitations, increased blood pressure
Very rare (less than ): Nausea
The most commonly reported side effects were discomfort or irritation in the nose, mouth or throat, and sneezing.
Very rare (less than ): Insomnia, nervousness, tremor, anxiety, restlessness, irritability, headache
