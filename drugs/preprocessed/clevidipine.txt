If any of the following side effects occur while taking clevidipine, check with your doctor or nurse immediately:
Cardiovascular side effects have included atrial fibrillation (up to ), myocardial infarction (less than ), cardiac arrest (less than ), systemic hypotension, and reflex tachycardia.
Hypotension and reflex tachycardia have been reported during postmarketing experience.
Gastrointestinal side effects have included nausea (up to ) and vomiting .
Ileus and nausea have been reported during postmarketing experience.
Renal side effects have included acute renal failure (up to ).
Nervous system side effects have included headache  and syncope (less than ).
Respiratory side effects have included dyspnea (less than ).
Other side effects have included decreased oxygen saturation (possible pulmonary shunting) during postmarketing experience.
Metabolic side effects have included increased blood triglycerides during postmarketing experience.
Hypersensitivity side effects have included hypersensitivity during postmarketing experience.
