Side effects include:
Skeletal muscle weakness.
Hypersensitivy side effects have included anaphylactic reaction, anaphylactoid reactions, bronchospasm, hypotension, tachycardia, acute urticaria and erythema.
Musculoskeletal side effects have included muscle weakness, prolonged skeletal muscle paralysis and muscle atrophy.
Respiratory side effects have included respiration insufficiency and apnea.
It is possible that some side effects of Norcuron may not have been reported.
These can be reported to the FDA here.
