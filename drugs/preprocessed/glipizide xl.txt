The majority of side effects have been dose related, transient, and have responded to dose reduction or withdrawal of the drug.
As with other sulfonylureas, some side effects associated with hypersensitivity may be severe and deaths have been reported in some instances.
Common ( to ): SyncopeUncommon (less than ): Arrhythmia, flushing, hypertension
Common ( to ): Sweating, pruritusUncommon (less than ): Rash, urticariaFrequency not reported: Rash, erythema, morbilliform or maculopapular eruptions, urticaria, pruritus, eczema
Dermatologic side effects frequently disappear with continued therapy.
Photosensitivity has been reported with other sulfonylureas.
Gastrointestinal side effects appear to be dose-related and may disappear on division or reduction of dosage.
