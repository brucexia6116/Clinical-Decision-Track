Commonly reported side effects of dalteparin include: pain at injection site.
Other side effects include: hematoma at injection site and increased serum alanine aminotransferase.
See below for a comprehensive list of adverse effects.
The most common side effects were any bleeding, postoperative transfusion, transaminase increases, and injection site pain.
