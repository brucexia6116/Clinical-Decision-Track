As well as its needed effects, dinoprostone topical (the active ingredient contained in Prostin E) may cause unwanted side effects that require medical attention.

Some dinoprostone topical side effects may not need any medical attention.
As your body gets used to the medicine these side effects may disappear.

This procedure may still result in some effects, which occur after the procedure is completed, that need medical attention.
