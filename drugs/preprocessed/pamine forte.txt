Side effects include:
Xerostomia,  decreased sweating,  adverse ocular effects (, blurred vision, mydriasis, cycloplegia, increased ocular tension).

Cardiovascular side effects have included tachycardia and palpitations.
Hypersensitivity side effects have included drug idiosyncrasies and anaphylaxis.
Nervous system side effects have included headache, nervousness, mental confusion, drowsiness, and dizziness.
Ocular side effects have included blurred vision, dilation of pupil, cycloplegia, and increased ocular tension.
Renal side effects have included urinary hesitancy and urinary retention.
Gastrointestinal side effects have included xerostomia, nausea, vomiting, constipation, and bloating.
Dermatologic side effects have included decreased sweating, urticaria, and other dermal manifestations.
Endocrine side effects have included suppression of lactation.
Genitourinary side effects have included impotence.
Other side effects have included weakness, insomnia, and loss of taste.
It is possible that some side effects of Pamine Forte may not have been reported.
These can be reported to the FDA here.
Always consult a healthcare professional for medical advice.
