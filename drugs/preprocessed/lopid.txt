Common side effects of Lopid include: malignant neoplasm, pancreatitis, dyspepsia, and cholelithiasis.
Other side effects include: abdominal pain.
See below for a comprehensive list of adverse effects.
The most frequently reported side effects were gastrointestinal reactions and included dyspepsia, abdominal pain, diarrhea, nausea, vomiting, acute appendicitis, constipation, and flatulence.
