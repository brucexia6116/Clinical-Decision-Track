As well as its needed effects, palonosetron may cause unwanted side effects that require medical attention.
Some palonosetron side effects may not need any medical attention.
As your body gets used to the medicine these side effects may disappear.
Your health care professional may be able to help you prevent or reduce these side effects, but do check with them if any of the following side effects continue, or if you are concerned about them:
Common ( to ): Bradycardia, hypotension, nonsustained tachycardiaUncommon ( to ): Hypertension, myocardial ischemia, extrasystoles, sinus tachycardia, sinus arrhythmia, supraventricular extrasystoles, QT prolongation
Uncommon ( to ): Allergic dermatitis, rash
Common ( to ): Constipation, diarrheaUncommon ( to ): Appetite decrease, dyspepsia, abdominal pain, dry mouth, hiccups, flatulence
Uncommon ( to ): Transient, asymptomatic increases in AST and/or ALT and bilirubin; increased transaminases
Common ( to ): HyperkalemiaUncommon ( to ): Electrolyte fluctuations, hyperglycemia, metabolic acidosis, glycosuria
Uncommon ( to ): Urinary retention
Common ( to ): AnxietyUncommon ( to ): Euphoric mood
Common ( to ): Headache, dizzinessUncommon ( to ): Fatigue, fever, hot flash, insomnia, somnolence, hypersomnia, paresthesia
Common ( to ): WeaknessUncommon ( to ): Arthralgia
Uncommon ( to ): Eye irritation, amblyopia
Very rare (less than ): Injection site reactions (burning, induration, discomfort, and pain)
Uncommon ( to ): Motion sickness, tinnitus, vein discoloration, vein distention, influenza-like syndrome
It is possible that some side effects of palonosetron may not have been reported.
These can be reported to the FDA here.
Always consult a healthcare professional for medical advice.
