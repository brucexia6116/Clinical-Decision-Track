No COMMON side effects have been reported with menthol cream.
Seek medical attention right away if any of these SEVERE side effects occur while taking menthol topical (the active ingredient contained in Sucrets Ice) 
Severe allergic reactions (rash; hives; itching; difficulty breathing; tightness in the chest; swelling of the mouth, face, lips, or tongue); redness or irritation at the application site.
Local side effects have included redness and irritation at site of application.
It is possible that some side effects of Sucrets Ice may not have been reported.
These can be reported to the FDA here.
Always consult a healthcare professional for medical advice.
