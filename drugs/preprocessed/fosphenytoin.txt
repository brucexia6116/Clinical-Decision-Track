Commonly reported side effects of fosphenytoin include: pruritus, ataxia, dizziness, drowsiness, nystagmus, burning sensation at injection site, and pain at injection site.
Other side effects include: stupor, hypotension, paresthesia, extrapyramidal reaction, headache, nausea, pelvic pain, tinnitus, tongue disease, vasodilatation, ecchymoses, and xerostomia.
See below for a comprehensive list of adverse effects.
If any of the following side effects occur while taking fosphenytoin, check with your doctor or nurse immediately:
