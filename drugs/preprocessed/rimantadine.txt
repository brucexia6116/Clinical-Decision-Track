Gastrointestinal and nervous system side effects are most frequently reported.
In a prophylaxis trial, 6.
9% of subjects discontinued treatment due to adverse reactions.
In geriatric patients, gastrointestinal and nervous system adverse effects occurred twice as often with rimantadine than with placebo.
Seizures were reported in a few patients during clinical trials with rimantadine.
In two patients, seizure activity had been absent for greater than 10 years.
However, their anticonvulsant medications had been withdrawn in the previous few months.
