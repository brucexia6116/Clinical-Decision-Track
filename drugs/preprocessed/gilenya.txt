Common side effects of Gilenya include: infection, back pain, diarrhea, increased serum alanine aminotransferase, increased serum aspartate aminotransferase, and increased liver enzymes.
Other side effects include: blurred vision, bradycardia, bronchitis, increased gamma-glutamyl transferase, lymphocytopenia, migraine, dizziness, dyspnea, hypertension, increased serum transaminases, and tinea.
See below for a comprehensive list of adverse effects.
