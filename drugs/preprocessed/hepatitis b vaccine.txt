Commonly reported side effects of hepatitis b adult vaccine include: fatigue, pain at injection site, and tenderness at injection site.
Other side effects include: dizziness, fever, headache, pruritus, vertigo, ecchymoses, erythema, induration at injection site, inflammation at injection site, and swelling at injection site.
See below for a comprehensive list of adverse effects.
The most common adverse events were injection site reactions, headache, and fatigue.
