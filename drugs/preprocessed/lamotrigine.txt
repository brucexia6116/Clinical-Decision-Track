Commonly reported side effects of lamotrigine include: ataxia, blurred vision, diplopia, dizziness, drowsiness, headache, insomnia, nausea, rhinitis, skin rash, tremor, vomiting, abdominal pain, and fever.
Other side effects include: dysmenorrhea, dyspepsia, vaginitis, abnormal gait, asthenia, bronchitis, constipation, pain, pruritus, and emotional lability.
