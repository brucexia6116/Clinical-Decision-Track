Applies to pancreatin: oral capsules, oral tablets
Side effects include:
Irritation of mucous membranes and respiratory tract,   diarrhea or other transient intestinal upset (with excessive dosage),  hyperuricosuria and hyperuricemia (with extremely high doses). 
It is possible that some side effects of pancreatin may not have been reported. These can be reported to the FDA here. Always consult a healthcare professional for medical advice.
