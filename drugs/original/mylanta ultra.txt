Generic Name: calcium carbonate / magnesium hydroxide
Applies to calcium carbonate / magnesium hydroxide: chewable tablets, suspension
 Check with your doctor if any of these most COMMON side effects persist or become bothersome:
Constipation; diarrhea; stomach upset.
Severe allergic reactions (rash; hives; itching; difficulty breathing; tightness in the chest; swelling of the mouth, face, lips, or tongue); loss of appetite; muscle weakness; nausea; slow reflexes; vomiting.


Not all side effects for Mylanta Ultra may be reported. You should always consult a doctor or healthcare professional for medical advice. Side effects can be reported to the FDA here.
