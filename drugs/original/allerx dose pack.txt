 Check with your doctor if any of these most COMMON side effects persist or become bothersome:
Blurred vision; dizziness; drowsiness; dry mouth, nose, or throat; excitability or irritability (especially in children); headache; lack of energy; lightheadedness; nausea; nervousness; trouble sleeping.
Severe allergic reactions (rash; hives; itching; difficulty breathing; tightness in the chest; swelling of the mouth, face, lips, or tongue); fast or irregular heartbeat; flushing; hallucinations; mental or mood changes (eg, anxiety); seizures; severe or persistent drowsiness or dizziness; tremor; trouble urinating; unusual tiredness or weakness.
Cardiovascular side effects have included tachycardia, palpitations, flushing, arrhythmias, and cardiovascular collapse with hypotension.
Nervous system side effects have included lassitude, drowsiness, giddiness, increased irritability or excitement, headache, fear, anxiety, nervousness,dizziness, restlessness, tremor, weakness, insomnia, hallucinations, convulsions, and CNS depression.
Ocular side effects have included blurred vision.
Gastrointestinal side effects have included nausea and excessive dryness of the nose, throat, and mouth.
Respiratory side effects have included respiratory difficulty.
Genitourinary side effects have included dysuria and urinary retention in patients with prostatic hypertrophy.
