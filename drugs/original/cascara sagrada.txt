Applies to cascara sagrada: capsules
 Check with your doctor if any of these most COMMON side effects persist or become bothersome:
Stomach upset.
Severe allergic reactions (rash; hives; itching; difficulty breathing; tightness in the chest; swelling of the mouth, face, lips, or tongue); diarrhea; failure to have a bowel movement; loose stools; rectal bleeding; stomach pain or cramps.


Not all side effects for cascara sagrada may be reported. You should always consult a doctor or healthcare professional for medical advice. Side effects can be reported to the FDA here.
