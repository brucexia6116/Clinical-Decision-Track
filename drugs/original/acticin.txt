More frequent side effects include: local pruritus.  See below for a comprehensive list of adverse effects.
Local side effects have included mild and transient burning and stinging usually associated with the severity of infestation.
Dermatological side effects have included pruritus (7%), erythema (1% to 2%), and rash (1% to 2%).
Nervous system side effects have included numbness and tingling (1% to 2%).  Headache and dizziness have also been reported.  Rare occurrences of seizure have been reported;  however, an association with the use of permethrin topical (the active ingredient contained in Acticin) has not been confirmed.
Gastrointestinal side effects have included abdominal pain, diarrhea, and nausea and/or vomiting.
General side effects have included fever.
