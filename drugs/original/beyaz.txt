Common side effects of Beyaz include: headache and spotting. Other side effects include: irregular menses and migraine.  See below for a comprehensive list of adverse effects.
Cardiovascular side effects have included hypertension, arterial or deep venous thrombotic events, strokes, and myocardial infarctions.
Genitourinary side effects have included breast tenderness, decreased libido, unscheduled (breakthrough or intracyclic) bleeding and spotting.
Gastrointestinal side effects have included nausea, vomiting, and inflammatory bowel disease.
Hepatic side effects have included jaundice, cholestasis and hepatic adenomas.
Metabolic side effects have included gallbladder disease, liver function disturbances, liver tumors, hyperkalemia, hypertriglyceridemia, changes in glucose tolerance, and effect on peripheral insulin resistance (including diabetes mellitus).
Nervous system side effects have included an increase in frequency or severity of migraine headaches.
Ocular side effects have included loss of vision, proptosis, diplopia, papilledema, or retinal vascular lesions.
Psychiatric side effects have included irritability, depression and affect lability.
Oncologic side effects have included carcinoma of the breasts and reproductive organs.
Musculoskeletal side effects have included systemic lupus erythematosus.
Dermatologic side effects have included chloasma, angioedema, erythema nodosum, and erythema multiforme.
Hypersensitivity side effects have included anaphylactic reaction.
