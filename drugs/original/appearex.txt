Generic Name: biotin
Applies to biotin: oral capsule, oral tablet
No side effects have been reported for biotin (the active ingredient contained in Appearex) in amounts up to 10 milligrams a day. However, check with your health care professional if you notice any unusual effects while you are taking it.
Not all side effects for Appearex may be reported. You should always consult a doctor or healthcare professional for medical advice. Side effects can be reported to the FDA here.
