Commonly reported side effects of rivastigmine include: abdominal pain, diarrhea, dizziness, headache, nausea, vomiting, weight loss, and anorexia. Other side effects include: abdominal pain, anxiety, asthenia, depression, dizziness, drowsiness, dyspepsia, fatigue, headache, tremor, anorexia, diaphoresis, flatulence, and malaise.
