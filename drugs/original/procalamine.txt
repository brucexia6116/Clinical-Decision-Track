Commonly reported side effects of cabazitaxel include: neutropenia, diarrhea, nausea, and vomiting. Other side effects include: febrile neutropenia, hypotension, and dehydration.  See below for a comprehensive list of adverse effects.
The most common adverse events were anemia, leukopenia, neutropenia, thrombocytopenia, and diarrhea.
