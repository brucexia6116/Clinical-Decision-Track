Commonly reported side effects of everolimus include: decreased hemoglobin, increased serum glucose, lymphocytopenia, and stomatitis. Other side effects include: pneumonitis, pneumonia, and increased hdl cholesterol.  See below for a comprehensive list of adverse effects.
