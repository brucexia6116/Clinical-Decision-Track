Generic Name: ascorbic acid / carbonyl iron
Applies to ascorbic acid / carbonyl iron: oral tablet
Get emergency medical help if you have any of these signs of an allergic reaction while taking ascorbic acid / carbonyl iron:  hives; difficult breathing; swelling of your face, lips, tongue, or throat.
Common side effects may include:
nausea, upset stomach;
constipation; or
diarrhea.
This is not a complete list of side effects and others may occur. Call your doctor for medical advice about side effects.
