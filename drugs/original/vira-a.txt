Generic Name: vidarabine ophthalmic
Applies to vidarabine ophthalmic: ophthalmic ointment, ophthalmic solution
As well as its needed effects, vidarabine ophthalmic (the active ingredient contained in Vira-A) may cause unwanted side effects that require medical attention. 
If any of the following side effects occur while taking vidarabine ophthalmic, check with your doctor or nurse as soon as possible:
Some vidarabine ophthalmic side effects may not need any medical attention. As your body gets used to the medicine these side effects may disappear. Your health care professional may be able to help you prevent or reduce these side effects, but do check with them if any of the following side effects continue, or if you are concerned about them:


It is possible that some side effects of Vira-A may not have been reported. These can be reported to the FDA here. Always consult a healthcare professional for medical advice.

Increased sensitivity of eyes to light
 itching, redness, swelling, pain, burning, or other sign of irritation not present before use of this medicine


Excess flow of tears
feeling of something in the eye
