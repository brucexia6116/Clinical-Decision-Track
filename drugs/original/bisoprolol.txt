The more commonly reported side effects observed with this drug are bradycardia, dizziness, and hypotension.[Ref]
Bradycardia occurred as a dose-related event.  Cardiac failure occurred in 18.4% of patients (n=1328) compared with 22.8% of placebo-treated patients (n=1321).[Ref]
