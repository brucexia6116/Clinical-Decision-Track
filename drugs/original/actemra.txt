The most common serious adverse reactions were serious infections.
Of the 2876 patients tested for anti-tocilizumab (the active ingredient contained in Actemra) antibodies in controlled clinical trials, 1.6% (46 patients) were positive; 5 patients experienced a hypersensitivity reaction that lead to treatment withdrawal.  However, in 1.1% (30 patients) of those who developed antibodies there was no apparent correlation to clinical response.
