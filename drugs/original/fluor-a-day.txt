Generic Name: fluoride
Applies to fluoride: chewable tablets
Other dosage forms:
 When used in small doses, no COMMON side effects have been reported with sodium fluoride (the active ingredient contained in Fluor-A-Day) chewable tablets. Seek medical attention right away if any of these SEVERE side effects occur while taking fluoride:
Severe allergic reactions (rash; hives; itching; difficulty breathing; tightness in the chest; swelling of the mouth, face, lips, or tongue).


Not all side effects for Fluor-A-Day may be reported. You should always consult a doctor or healthcare professional for medical advice. Side effects can be reported to the FDA here.
