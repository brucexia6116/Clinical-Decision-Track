Commonly reported side effects of fluticasone include: oral candidiasis, upper respiratory tract infection, nasal congestion, and pharyngitis. Other side effects include: oral candidiasis, back disorder, diarrhea, influenza, rhinitis, upper respiratory tract inflammation, voice disorder, and hoarseness.  See below for a comprehensive list of adverse effects.
