Commonly reported side effects of taliglucerase alfa include: arthralgia, back pain, and headache. Other side effects include: asthenia, chest discomfort, chest pain, fatigue, increased blood pressure, urticaria, erythema, and flushing.  See below for a comprehensive list of adverse effects.
