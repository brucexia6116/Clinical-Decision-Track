 Check with your doctor if any of these most COMMON side effects persist or become bothersome:
Diarrhea; dizziness; drowsiness; excitability; headache; irritability; nausea; stomach upset; trouble sleeping.
Severe allergic reactions (rash; hives; itching; difficulty breathing; tightness in the chest; swelling of the mouth, face, lips, or tongue); difficulty urinating; fast, slow, or irregular heartbeat; fever; hallucinations; mental or mood changes (eg, anxiety, nervousness); paleness; seizures; severe or persistent dizziness, lightheadedness, or headache; tremor.
Cardiovascular side effects have included palpitations, tachycardia, and cardiovascular collapse and death.
Gastrointestinal side effects have included nausea and vomiting.
Nervous system side effects have included nervousness, dizziness, sleeplessness, light-headedness, tremor, hallucinations, convulsions, CNS depression, fear, anxiety, headache, and increased irritability or excitement.
Respiratory side effects have included respiratory difficulties.
General side effects have included weakness and slight drowsiness.
