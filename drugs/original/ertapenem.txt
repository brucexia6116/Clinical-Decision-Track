In general, the most common side effects associated with ertapenem have been considered mild to moderate and have included diarrhea (5.5%), infused vein complication (3.7%), nausea (3.1%), headache (2.2%), and vaginitis in females (2.1%).[Ref]
Small intestinal obstruction, C difficile infection or colitis, dry mouth, and hematochezia were reported in a study using 1 g of ertapenem for prophylaxis of surgical site infection following elective colorectal surgery.[Ref]
