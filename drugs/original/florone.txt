Generic Name: diflorasone topical
Applies to diflorasone topical: topical application cream, topical application ointment
In addition to its needed effects, some unwanted effects may be caused by diflorasone topical (the active ingredient contained in Florone). In the event that any of these side effects do occur, they may require medical attention.

Some of the side effects that can occur with diflorasone topical may not need medical attention. As your body adjusts to the medicine during treatment these side effects may go away. Your health care professional may also be able to tell you about ways to reduce or prevent some of these side effects.
