Applies to acetaminophen / caffeine / chlorpheniramine / hydrocodone / phenylephrine: oral tablet
Cardiovascular side effects have included hypertension, postural hypotension, tachycardia, and palpitations.[Ref]
Genitourinary side effects have included ureteral spasm, spasm of vesicle sphincters, and urinary retention.[Ref]
Nervous system side effects have included sedation, drowsiness, mental clouding, lethargy, and impairment of mental and physical performance.[Ref]
Psychiatric side effects have included anxiety, fear, dysphoria, psychic dependence, and mood changes.[Ref]
Ocular side effects have included blurred vision.[Ref]
Gastrointestinal side effects have included nausea and vomiting.[Ref]
Respiratory side effects have included dose-related respiratory depression.[Ref]
