Cardiovascular side effects have included hypertension, postural hypotension, tachycardia, and palpitations.
Genitourinary side effects have included ureteral spasm, spasm of vesicle sphincters, and urinary retention.
Nervous system side effects have included sedation, drowsiness, mental clouding, lethargy, and impairment of mental and physical performance.
Psychiatric side effects have included anxiety, fear, dysphoria, psychic dependence, and mood changes.
Ocular side effects have included blurred vision.
Gastrointestinal side effects have included nausea and vomiting.
Respiratory side effects have included dose-related respiratory depression.
