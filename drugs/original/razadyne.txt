Common side effects of Razadyne include: nausea and vomiting. Other side effects include: dizziness, dyspepsia, fatigue, headache, weight loss, diarrhea, and anorexia.  See below for a comprehensive list of adverse effects.
As well as its needed effects, galantamine (the active ingredient contained in Razadyne) may cause unwanted side effects that require medical attention.
