In animal studies, coal tar has been shown to increase the chance of skin cancer.
As well as its needed effects, coal tar topical (the active ingredient contained in Oxipor VHC) may cause unwanted side effects that require medical attention. 
Some coal tar topical side effects may not need any medical attention. As your body gets used to the medicine these side effects may disappear.
