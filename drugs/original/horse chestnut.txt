Applies to horse chestnut: capsules, controlled-release capsules
 Check with your doctor if any of these most COMMON side effects persist or become bothersome:
Dizziness; nausea; stomach irritation; vomiting.
Severe allergic reactions (rash; hives; itching; difficulty breathing; tightness in the chest; swelling of the mouth, face, lips, or tongue); fatigue; fever; yellowing of the skin or eyes.


Not all side effects for horse chestnut may be reported. You should always consult a doctor or healthcare professional for medical advice. Side effects can be reported to the FDA here.
