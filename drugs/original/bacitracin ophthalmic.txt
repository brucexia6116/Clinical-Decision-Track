Applies to bacitracin ophthalmic: ophthalmic ointment
Get emergency medical help if you have any of these signs of an allergic reaction while taking bacitracin ophthalmic:  hives; difficult breathing; swelling of your face, lips, tongue, or throat.
Less serious side effects may include temporary blurred vision.
This is not a complete list of side effects and others may occur. Call your doctor for medical advice about side effects. 
Not all side effects for bacitracin ophthalmic may be reported. You should always consult a doctor or healthcare professional for medical advice. Side effects can be reported to the FDA here.
