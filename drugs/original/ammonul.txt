Common side effects of Ammonul include: infection, respiratory tract disease, central nervous system disease, disorder of lymphatic system, hematologic disease, nutrition disorder, and vomiting. Other side effects include: cerebral edema, convulsions, metabolic acidosis, vascular disease, altered mental status, anemia, cardiac disorder, disorder of integument, fever, hyperammonemia, hyperglycemia, hypokalemia, hypotension, mental disorders, and renal disease.  See below for a comprehensive list of adverse effects.
If any of the following side effects occur while taking sodium benzoate / sodium phenylacetate, check with your doctor or nurse immediately:
-The most frequently reported adverse reactions (incidence 6% or greater) are vomiting, hyperglycemia, hypokalemia, convulsions, and mental impairment.-Patients 30 days of age or younger had more blood and lymphatic system disorders and vascular disorders (specifically hypotension), while patients older than 30 days had more GI disorders (specifically nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea).
