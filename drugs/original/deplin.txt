Generic Name: l-methylfolate
Applies to l-methylfolate: capsules, tablets
 No COMMON side effects have been reported with l-methylfolate (the active ingredient contained in Deplin)  Seek medical attention right away if any of these SEVERE side effects occur while taking l-methylfolate:
Severe allergic reactions (rash; hives; itching; difficulty breathing; tightness in the chest; swelling of the mouth, face, lips, or tongue).


Applies to l-methylfolate: oral capsule, oral tablet, oral tablet extended release
