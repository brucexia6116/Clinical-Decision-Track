Gastrointestinal side effects have included nausea (26%), vomiting (13%), constipation (12.5%), diarrhea (12.3%), abdominal pain (11.8%), and anorexia (9%).[Ref]
Hematologic side effects have included anemia (4.5%) and postmarketing reports of leukopenia.[Ref]
Cardiovascular side effects have included vasodilation (17.7%), and peripheral edema (9%).  Postmarketing reports have included thromboembolic phenomena.[Ref]
Musculoskeletal side effects have included bone pain (15.8%), and arthritis (2.8%).  Postmarketing reports have included myalgia.[Ref]
Nervous system side effects have included dizziness (6.9%), insomnia (6.9%), paresthesia (6.4%), depression (5.7%), and anxiety (5%).  Postmarketing reports have included vertigo.[Ref]
Respiratory side effects have included pharyngitis (16.1%), dyspnea (14.9%), and cough increased (10.4%).[Ref]
Genitourinary side effects have included urinary tract infection (6.1%).  Postmarketing reports have included vaginal bleeding.[Ref]
Dermatologic side effects have included rash (7.3%).[Ref]
Endocrine side effects have included sweating (5%).[Ref]
General side effects have included asthenia (22.7%), pain (18.9%), headache (15.4%), back pain (14.4%), pain at injection site (10.9%), pelvic pain (9.9%), chest pain (7.1%), flu syndrome (7.1%), fever (6.4%), and accidental injury (4.5%).[Ref]
Hypersensitivity side effects including angioedema and urticaria have been reported.[Ref]
Hepatic side effects have included postmarketing reports of elevated bilirubin, elevated gamma GT, hepatitis, and liver failure.
