Generic Name: sertaconazole topical
Applies to sertaconazole topical: topical cream
Some of the side effects that can occur with sertaconazole topical (the active ingredient contained in Ertaczo) may not need medical attention. As your body adjusts to the medicine during treatment these side effects may go away. Your health care professional may also be able to tell you about ways to reduce or prevent some of these side effects.
