Commonly reported side effects of gabapentin enacarbil include: ataxia, dizziness, drowsiness, fatigue, fever, nystagmus, sedation, and viral infection. Other side effects include: blurred vision, diplopia, peripheral edema, tremor, amblyopia, irritability, and xerostomia.  See below for a comprehensive list of adverse effects.

In addition to its needed effects, some unwanted effects may be caused by gabapentin enacarbil. In the event that any of these side effects do occur, they may require medical attention.
