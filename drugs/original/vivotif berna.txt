As well as its needed effects, typhoid vaccine, live (the active ingredient contained in Vivotif Berna) may cause unwanted side effects that require medical attention.

Some typhoid vaccine, live side effects may not need any medical attention. As your body gets used to the medicine these side effects may disappear.
