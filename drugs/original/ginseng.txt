Applies to ginseng: capsules, tablets
 Check with your doctor if any of these most COMMON side effects persist or become bothersome:
Agitation; diarrhea; headache; nervousness; trouble sleeping.
Severe allergic reactions (rash; hives; itching; difficulty breathing; tightness in the chest; swelling of the mouth, face, lips, or tongue); vaginal bleeding.


Not all side effects for ginseng may be reported. You should always consult a doctor or healthcare professional for medical advice. Side effects can be reported to the FDA here.
