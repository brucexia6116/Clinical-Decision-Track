Applies to salsalate: oral tablets
Side effects include:
Tinnitus, nausea, hearing impairment, rash, vertigo. 
Applies to salsalate: oral capsule, oral tablet
Gastrointestinal side effects including abdominal pain, diarrhea, nausea, and GI bleeding have been frequently reported.[Ref]
Hepatic side effects including abnormal hepatic function and hepatitis have been reported.[Ref]
Cardiovascular side effects including angioedema and hypotension have been reported.[Ref]
Renal side effects including decreased creatinine clearance, nephritis, and urticaria have been reported.[Ref]
Respiratory side effects including bronchospasm have been reported.[Ref]
Other side effects including tinnitus, hearing impairment, vertigo, rash, and anaphylactic shock have been reported.[Ref]
