The most common side effects include headache, constipation, sleep disorders, and dizziness.[Ref]
Loss of consciousness may occur immediately/closely after an IV bolus in patients receiving treatment for prophylaxis of chemotherapy induced nausea and vomiting.Headache and dizziness occurred more frequently at higher doses.[Ref]
