Common side effects of Engerix-B include: fatigue, pain at injection site, and tenderness at injection site. Other side effects include: dizziness, fever, headache, pruritus, vertigo, ecchymoses, erythema, induration at injection site, inflammation at injection site, and swelling at injection site.  See below for a comprehensive list of adverse effects.

In addition to its needed effects, some unwanted effects may be caused by hepatitis b adult vaccine (the active ingredient contained in Engerix-B). In the event that any of these side effects do occur, they may require medical attention.
