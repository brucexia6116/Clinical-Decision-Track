Generic Name: tolnaftate topical
Applies to tolnaftate topical: topical cream, topical gel/jelly, topical lotion, topical ointment, topical powder, topical solution, topical spray
As well as its needed effects, tolnaftate topical (the active ingredient contained in Tinactin) may cause unwanted side effects that require medical attention. 
If any of the following side effects occur while taking tolnaftate topical, check with your doctor or nurse as soon as possible:
When you apply the aerosol solution form of this medicine, a mild temporary stinging may be expected.
