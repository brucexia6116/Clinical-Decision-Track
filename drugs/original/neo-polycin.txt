As well as its needed effects, bacitracin / neomycin / polymyxin b ophthalmic may cause unwanted side effects that require medical attention. 
After application, eye ointments usually cause your vision to blur for a few minutes.
Hypersensitivity reactions including itching, swelling and conjunctival erythema have been reported.  Anaphylaxis and other more serious hypersensitivity reactions have been reported rarely.
Local side effects have included irritation upon administration.
It is possible that some side effects of Neo-Polycin may not have been reported. These can be reported to the FDA here. Always consult a healthcare professional for medical advice.
Itching, rash, redness, swelling, or other sign of irritation not present before use of this medicine
