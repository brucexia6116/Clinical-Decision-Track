Generic Name: spectinomycin
Applies to spectinomycin: parenteral powder for im injection
Side effects include:
Pain at the injection site,  urticaria,  transient rash, pruritus, dizziness,  headache, nausea,  vomiting, chills,  fever,  nervousness, insomnia.
