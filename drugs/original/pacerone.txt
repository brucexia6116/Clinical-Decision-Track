Common side effects of Pacerone include: hypersensitivity pneumonitis, interstitial pneumonitis, pulmonary toxicity, blurred vision, peripheral neuropathy, phototoxicity, and visual halos around lights.  See below for a comprehensive list of adverse effects.

As well as its needed effects, amiodarone (the active ingredient contained in Pacerone) may cause unwanted side effects that require medical attention.
