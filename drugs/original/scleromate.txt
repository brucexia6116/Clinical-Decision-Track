Cardiovascular side effects have included thrombosis, valvular incompetency, and vascular collapse.
Nervous system side effects have included drowsiness, headache, and dizziness.
Dermatologic side effects have included urticaria.
Gastrointestinal side effects have included nausea and vomiting.
Local side effects have included burning at the site of injection and severe extravasation effects.
Musculoskeletal side effects have included weakness.
Respiratory side effects have included asthma and pulmonary embolism.
Hypersensitivity side effects have included anaphylaxis reactions.
It is possible that some side effects of Scleromate may not have been reported. These can be reported to the FDA here. Always consult a healthcare professional for medical advice.
