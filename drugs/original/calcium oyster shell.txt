 No COMMON side effects have been reported with calcium carbonate (the active ingredient contained in Calcium Oyster Shell) chewable tablets. Seek medical attention right away if any of these SEVERE side effects occur while taking calcium carbonate:
Severe allergic reactions (rash; hives; itching; difficulty breathing; tightness in the chest; swelling of the mouth, face, lips, or tongue).
