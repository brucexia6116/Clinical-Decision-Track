Side effects include:
Xerostomia,  decreased sweating,  adverse ophthalmic effects (e.g., blurred vision, mydriasis, cycloplegia, increased ocular tension). 
Gastrointestinal side effects have included nausea, vomiting, constipation, bloating, and drying of salivary secretions.
Cardiovascular side effects have included tachycardia and palpitations.
Nervous system side effects have included headache, nervousness, and mental confusion.
Hypersensitivity reactions have included allergic reactions or drug idiosyncrasies, anaphylaxis, urticaria, and other dermal manifestations.
Genitourinary side effects have included urinary hesitancy, urinary retention, and impotence.
Ocular side effects have included blurred vision, mydriasis, cycloplegia, and increased ocular tension.
Endocrine side effects have included suppression of lactation.
General side effects have included decreased sweating, drowsiness, weakness, dizziness, and insomnia.
It is possible that some side effects of propantheline may not have been reported. These can be reported to the FDA here. Always consult a healthcare professional for medical advice.
