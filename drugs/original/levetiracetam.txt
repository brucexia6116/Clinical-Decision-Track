Commonly reported side effects of levetiracetam include: infection, psychoneurosis, drowsiness, asthenia, headache, nasopharyngitis, nervousness, abnormal behavior, aggressive behavior, agitation, anxiety, apathy, depersonalization, depression, fatigue, hostility, hyperkinesia, mental disorders, outbursts of anger, personality disorder, emotional lability, irritability, laceration, and mood changes. Other side effects include: tonic clonic epilepsy, dizziness, vertigo, abnormal behavior, decreased neutrophils, depressed mood, influenza, nasopharyngitis, neck pain, and pain.
