Common side effects of Depakote include: abdominal pain, asthenia, dizziness, drowsiness, nausea, and anorexia. Other side effects include: abnormality in thinking, alopecia, ataxia, nystagmus, tremor, weight loss, fever, and skin rash.  See below for a comprehensive list of adverse effects.
