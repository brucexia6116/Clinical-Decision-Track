If any of the following side effects occur while taking protein c, check with your doctor or nurse immediately:
Immunologic side effects have included hypersensitivity or allergic reactions (itching and rash) and lightheadedness.
Respiratory side effects have included hemothorax.
Nervous system side effects have included fever and restlessness.
Cardiovascular side effects have included hypotension.
Metabolic side effects have included hyperhidrosis.
