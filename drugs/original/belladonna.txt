Applies to belladonna: oral belladonna alkaloids with phenobarbital elixir and immediate- and extended-release tablets, oral belladonna extract, oral belladonna leaf, oral belladonna tincture, rectal suppositories
Side effects include:
Drowsiness,     xerostomia,      tachycardia,      palpitation,     dizziness,     nervousness,    urinary hesitancy and retention,      decreased sweating,     constipation,     increased ocular tension,    photophobia,   blurred vision,      mydriasis.    
Not all side effects for belladonna may be reported. You should always consult a doctor or healthcare professional for medical advice. Side effects can be reported to the FDA here.
