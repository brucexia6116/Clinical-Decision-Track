Commonly reported side effects of asparaginase erwinia chrysanthemi include: anaphylaxis. Other side effects include: pancreatitis and severe hypersensitivity.  See below for a comprehensive list of adverse effects.
If any of the following side effects occur while taking asparaginase erwinia chrysanthemi, check with your doctor or nurse immediately:
Most common adverse reactions were systemic hypersensitivity, hyperglycemia, abnormal transaminases, fever, pancreatitis, local reactions, vomiting, nausea, thrombosis, hyperbilirubinemia, abdominal pain/discomfort, and diarrhea.
