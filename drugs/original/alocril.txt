Some of the side effects that can occur with nedocromil ophthalmic (the active ingredient contained in Alocril) may not need medical attention. As your body adjusts to the medicine during treatment these side effects may go away. Your health care professional may also be able to tell you about ways to reduce or prevent some of these side effects.
