Commonly reported side effects of lubiprostone include: diarrhea, headache, and nausea. Other side effects include: urinary tract infection, abdominal distention, abdominal pain, dizziness, peripheral edema, sinusitis, vomiting, and flatulence.  See below for a comprehensive list of adverse effects.
