Generic Name: dextromethorphan / guaifenesin / potassium guaiacolsulfonate
Applies to dextromethorphan / guaifenesin / potassium guaiacolsulfonate: sustained-release capsules
 Check with your doctor if any of these most COMMON side effects persist or become bothersome:
Dizziness; headache; nausea; upset stomach; vomiting.
Severe allergic reactions (rash; hives; difficulty breathing; tightness in the chest; swelling of the mouth, face, lips, or tongue); lower back or side pain.


Not all side effects for Humibid DM may be reported. You should always consult a doctor or healthcare professional for medical advice. Side effects can be reported to the FDA here.
