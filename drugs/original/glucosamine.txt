Get emergency medical help if you have any of these signs of an allergic reaction while taking glucosamine:  hives; difficult breathing; swelling of your face, lips, tongue, or throat.
Common side effects may include:
nausea, vomiting;
diarrhea, constipation; or
heartburn.
This is not a complete list of side effects and others may occur. Call your doctor for medical advice about side effects.
