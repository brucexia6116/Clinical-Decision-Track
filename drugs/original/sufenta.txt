Generic Name: sufentanil
Applies to sufentanil: parenteral injection
Side effects include:
Respiratory depression,   skeletal muscle rigidity (e.g., truncal muscles, neck, extremities).    
Applies to sufentanil: compounding powder, injectable solution
Respiratory side effects including respiratory depression, apnea, and bronchospasm have been reported frequently.[Ref]
Cardiovascular side effects including hypotension, hypertension, bradycardia, cardiac arrest, and arrhythmias have been reported infrequently.[Ref]
Musculoskeletal side effects including skeletal muscle rigidity, particularly of the truncal muscles, intraoperative muscle movement, and chest wall rigidity have been reported frequently.[Ref]
Gastrointestinal side effects including nausea and vomiting have been reported.[Ref]
Genitourinary side effects including urinary retention have been reported commonly.[Ref]
Dermatologic side effects including pruritus and erythema have been reported.[Ref]
General side effects including chills and somnolence have been reported.[Ref]
