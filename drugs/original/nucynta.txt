Generic Name: tapentadol
Common side effects of Nucynta include: drowsiness, nausea, and vomiting. Other side effects include: constipation, pruritus, and xerostomia.  See below for a comprehensive list of adverse effects.
Applies to tapentadol: oral solution, oral tablet, oral tablet extended release
As well as its needed effects, tapentadol (the active ingredient contained in Nucynta) may cause unwanted side effects that require medical attention.
