Generic Name: carbetapentane / phenylephrine / pyrilamine
Applies to carbetapentane / phenylephrine / pyrilamine: suspension, tablets
 Check with your doctor if any of these most COMMON side effects persist or become bothersome:
Constipation; diarrhea; dizziness; drowsiness; excitability; headache; loss of appetite; nausea; nervousness or anxiety; trouble sleeping; upset stomach; vomiting; weakness.
Severe allergic reactions (rash; hives; difficulty breathing; tightness in the chest; swelling of the mouth, face, lips, or tongue); difficulty urinating or inability to urinate; fast or irregular heartbeat; hallucinations; seizures; severe dizziness, lightheadedness, or headache; tremor; trouble sleeping; vision changes.


Applies to carbetapentane / phenylephrine / pyrilamine: oral suspension, oral tablet
Nervous system side effects have included drowsiness and sedation.[Ref]
Gastrointestinal side effects have included upset stomach and dry mucous membranes.[Ref]
Respiratory side effects have included respiratory difficulty.[Ref]
General side effects have included weakness and pallor.[Ref]
Cardiovascular side effects have included arrhythmias, increased heart rate, and increased blood pressure.[Ref]
