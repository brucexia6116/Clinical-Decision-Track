As well as its needed effects, cholecalciferol (the active ingredient contained in Replesta) may cause unwanted side effects that require medical attention. 
If any of the following side effects occur while taking cholecalciferol, check with your doctor immediately:
Metabolic side effects have included hypercalcemia.
Uncommon (0.1% to 1%): Hypercalciuria
Rare (less than 0.1%): Pruritus, rash, urticaria
Frequency not reported: Hypersensitivity reactions such as angioedema or laryngeal edema
Frequency not reported: Nausea, vomiting
It is possible that some side effects of Replesta may not have been reported. These can be reported to the FDA here. Always consult a healthcare professional for medical advice.
