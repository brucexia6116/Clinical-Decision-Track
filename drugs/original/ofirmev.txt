As well as its needed effects, acetaminophen (the active ingredient contained in Ofirmev) may cause unwanted side effects that require medical attention. 
In general, acetaminophen (the active ingredient contained in Ofirmev) is well-tolerated when administered in therapeutic doses.  The most commonly reported adverse reactions have included nausea, vomiting, constipation.  Injection site pain and injection site reaction have been reported with the IV product.
