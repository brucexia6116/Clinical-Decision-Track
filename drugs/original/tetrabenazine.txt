Commonly reported side effects of tetrabenazine include: drowsiness, sedation, bradykinesia, hypertonia, muscle rigidity, depression, exacerbation of depression, akathisia, and restlessness. Other side effects include: dysphagia, anxiety, and dizziness.  See below for a comprehensive list of adverse effects.
As well as its needed effects, tetrabenazine may cause unwanted side effects that require medical attention.

Some tetrabenazine side effects may not need any medical attention. As your body gets used to the medicine these side effects may disappear.
