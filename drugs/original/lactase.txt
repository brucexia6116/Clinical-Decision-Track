 When used in small doses, no COMMON side effects have been reported with lactase chewable tablets. Seek medical attention right away if any of these SEVERE side effects occur while taking lactase:
Severe allergic reactions (rash; hives; difficulty breathing; tightness in the chest; swelling of the mouth, face, lips, or tongue).
In general, side effects have not been reported with the use of lactase.
