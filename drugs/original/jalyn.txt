Cardiovascular side effects have included chest pain, orthostatic hypotension, and palpitations.
Gastrointestinal side effects have included diarrhea, constipation and vomiting.
Immunologic side effects have included hypersensitivity reactions, including rash, urticaria, pruritus, angioedema, localized edema, serious skin reactions, and respiratory problems.
Ocular side effects have included a variant of small pupil syndrome known as Intraoperative floppy iris syndrome (IFIS) during cataract surgery.
General side effects have included infection.
Respiratory side effects have included rhinitis, pharyngitis, increased cough and sinusitis.
Musculoskeletal side effects have included back pain.
Postmarketing psychiatric side effects have included depressed mood.
