Applies to chlophedianol / guaifenesin: liquid
 Check with your doctor if any of these most COMMON side effects persist or become bothersome:
Dizziness; drowsiness; nausea; stomach upset.
Severe allergic reactions (rash; hives; itching; difficulty breathing; tightness in the chest; swelling of the mouth, face, lips, or tongue).


Not all side effects for chlophedianol / guaifenesin may be reported. You should always consult a doctor or healthcare professional for medical advice. Side effects can be reported to the FDA here.
