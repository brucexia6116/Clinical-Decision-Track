Generic Name: multivitamin with fluoride
Applies to multivitamin with fluoride: drops
 No COMMON side effects have been reported with multivitamins with l-methylfolate and fluoride drops. Seek medical attention right away if any of these SEVERE side effects occur while taking multivitamin with fluoride (the active ingredient contained in Tri-Vi-Flor) 
Severe allergic reactions (rash; hives; itching; difficulty breathing; tightness in the chest; swelling of the mouth, face, lips, or tongue).
