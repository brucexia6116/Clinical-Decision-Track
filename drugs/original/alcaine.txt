If any of the following side effects occur while taking proparacaine ophthalmic, check with your doctor or nurse immediately:
Blurred vision
redness of the clear part of the eye
sensitivity to light
severe stinging in the eye
tearing
throbbing eye pain
Bloody eye
burning, stinging, itching, redness, or irritation of the eye
change in vision
Dry skin with cracking
grooves or lines in the skin of the fingertips
skin rash, hives, itching, or redness
